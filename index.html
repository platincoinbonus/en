    What is the blockchain?

    The blockchain is a revolutionary data storage and transmission technology that is the next generation of the Internet. It has enormous potential for any business, any society, and every individual and will have the greatest impact on the global economy in the coming decade.

    If the internet is a repository of information, then the blockchain is an internet of values that can be stored in a global distributed register. Such a register cannot be forged or cheated, it can be checked by everyone, and, at the same time, no one can change anything in it. It's just a genius technology!

    How to start minting with the
    Power Minter?

    The main application in the Power Minter is the PLC Farm where the entire minting process takes place. So, here's what you need to start the minting process:

    • Log into the PLC Farm, create a Farm wallet, send coins to it, and sign a smart contract.

    • After that, the PLC Wallet app will mint equal amounts of PLCs on your main wallet every month!
